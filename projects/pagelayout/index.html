What makes good movies?

Movies truly entertain our eyes and ears. But some go beyond just being visually and audibly pleasing; they touch our hearts deeply. When a film’s visuals, music, script, and acting come together to create a lasting impact, we call it a masterpiece.

Here, I’ve gathered films that I confidently consider masterpieces. Along with the movie posters, I’ve shared a few thoughts on why these films stand out. Trust me, and I highly recommend giving them a watch.
