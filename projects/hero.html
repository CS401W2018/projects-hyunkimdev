Paul Muad'dib Atreides is a main character in the "Dune" series by Frank Herbert. He is the son of Duke Leto and Lady Jessica. Paul becomes a strong leader and leads a big fight on the desert planet, Arrakis. He is known for seeing the future and is very wise in politics, making him a hero to the people living there, called Fremen.
