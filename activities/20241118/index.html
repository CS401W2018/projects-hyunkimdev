What is DNS?

DNS changes website names (like google.com) into numbers (IP addresses) computers use.

How do you get a domain name?

Buy it from a website like GoDaddy or Namecheap.

What is SSL?

SSL keeps your data safe by making a secure connection.

How do you get a security certificate?

Buy it or get it free from Let’s Encrypt.

How does SSL work?

It makes a safe link and hides your data.

How do you stay safe online?

Use strong passwords, don’t click bad links, and update your apps.
